Teaching Experience



    The second half of my Undergraduate experience has been shaped heavily by my role as a Teaching Assistant for DATA 301: Applied Machine Learning. Through this work, I have been able to put my passion for teaching others to use in a way that can help others.


    I am responsible for holding office hours 1-2 times per week for around 2 hours each time and often hold multiple extra sessions to offer students additional help and accommodate everyone's specific schedules and needs.
